PROJECT INFO

Madeleine Dalla is a Swedish, Nicaragua born photographer based in New York. To frame her incredible work we built her a visual identity accompanied by an elegant new portfolio website. With this project, we didn’t want to overwhelm her work with art direction and motion but strip it free from these elements and delicately accentuate her photography. www.madeleinedalla.com
