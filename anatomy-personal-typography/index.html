PROJECT INFO

A project to combine typography and symbols in space with the anatomy of humans and animals. The key of the project is to think with type and symbols on the grounds of anatomy-concepts. “… our 'Physick' and 'Anatomy' have embraced such infinite varieties of being, have laid open such new worlds in time and space, have grappled, not unsuccessfully, with such complex problems, that the eyes of Vesaliusand of Harvey might be dazzled by the sight of the tree that has grown out of their grain of mustard seed.” - Thomas Henry Huxley, Lay Sermons, Addresses, and Reviews
