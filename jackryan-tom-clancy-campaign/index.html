PROJECT DETAILS

Experience what it's like to be on the ground with Jack Ryan using Facebook messenger. Talk to Jack as you make choices that will affect the outcome of the mission. Track down your enemy through international cities, go on an epic car chase, and finally stel out of messanger and into a drone surveillance mobile game that uses your gyroscope to immerse you in action.
