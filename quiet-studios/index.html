Quiet Studios

PROJECT DETAILS

Quiet combines edgy provocative high fashion with bondage themes in a very clean and minimalistic way. Every piece of the collections is crafted in a personal process with best care of the materials. Creating a new zone for clothing with an atmosphere of dark concrete and steel. The basic idea of Quiet is to give women and men a rough futuristic and although tender look with her fashion. The goal was to translate the story behind Quiet into a digital branding, website and cohesive look that showcases the modern and precise style of Quiet Studio.
