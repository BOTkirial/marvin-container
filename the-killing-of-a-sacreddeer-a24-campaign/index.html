PROJECT INFO

To get fans and moviegoers intrigued about this movie Watson DG created a sense of mystery around the film’s premise with pieces that raise more questions than they answer. The campaign will create intrigue both conceptually and stylistically, through cryptic copy lines and a visual style that features distortion, stretching pixels, and glitch art.
