PROJECT DETAILS

Yuniqu is a perfume subscription service that allows you to purchase a new scent every month, therefore opening the customer's mind to the world of perfumes. I was asked to create a corporate design and website for the brand. Using soft color systems, unusual layouts based on grid systems I created specifically for this project, and a sans serif font with visible contrast (Chap - Schick Toikka) I tried to capture the unique essence of the brand and bring it to life. To capture Yuniqu's soul I teamed up with photographer Peter Muntanion who guided me through the process of shooting the campaign, and product images for the brand.
